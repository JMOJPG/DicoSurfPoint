Things to Do

Surf lessons, reef exploring, scenic local hikes, fishing trips, and much more await you in the untouched paradise of Simeulue. Whether you're paddling out for your first wave, snorkeling vibrant coral reefs, trekking through lush tropical trails, or chasing an epic sunset from the shore, there's something here for every kind of traveler. Our experiences are guided by locals who know the island inside and out, offering you a deeper connection to both nature and culture.
