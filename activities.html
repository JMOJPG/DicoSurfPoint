Things to Do

Surf lessons, reef exploring, scenic local hikes, yoga sessions, and much more await you in the untouched paradise of Simeulue. Whether you're paddling out for your first wave, snorkeling vibrant coral reefs, trekking through lush tropical trails, or unwinding with a sunset yoga class, there's something here for every kind of traveler. Our experiences are guided by locals who know the island inside and out, offering you a deeper connection to both nature and culture. Adventure, relaxation, and discovery all come together on this remote and magical island.
