About Dico Surf Point

Nestled on the untouched island of Simeulue, Dico Surf Point is more than just a place to stay — it's an experience. Named in honour of a loyal Great Dane owned by the owner of the lodge, our lodge carries that same spirit of freedom, adventure, and companionship. Whether you're chasing perfect uncrowded world class waves or just need a place to unwind, Dico Surf Point welcomes you.
